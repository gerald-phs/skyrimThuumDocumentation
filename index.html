Introduction

Dragons are ancient creatures. Very intelligent, they devised a language of their own. Ever since being a small dragonling, the Voice is already in their bones. It is a very terse dialect, composed of monosyllabic words, raw in their pronunciation: almost like incantations. I have never met a person who has mastered the Voice, and I still believe none but the High Monks can. I once thought about climbing the thousand steps, but I did not, and never will. Leave the pride to the doer of deeds and the doer of works. I write stuff down and nothing more. However, if you wish to know more about Draconic, read on.
